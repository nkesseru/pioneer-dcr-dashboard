What disqualifies the pack on intake

Reviewers refuse to begin reading the pack if any of these are true:

  • Fewer than 8 of the 10 rows have a link or path filled in
  • Any of the 8 Vital Read recordings is missing or unviewable
  • Loop entry log has zero closed Loops
  • Customer Economics analysis cannot be opened or the math isn't visible

Refusal at intake is not a verdict on the candidate — it's a defense of the reviewer's time. The candidate gets one re-submission window of 7 days.

What reviewers verify

For each row, the reviewer fills the verified column with one of:

  • Y — artifact present, meets bar
  • N — artifact present, does not meet bar (cite reason in margin)
  • Missing — no artifact provided

The verified column is reproduced in the Operator I reviewer scorecard. Discrepancies between the three reviewers' verified columns are the primary input to the Week 6 calibration meeting.

Reviewer hand-off

When the candidate submits this index, three reviewers receive:

  1. This filled-in index
  2. The artifacts themselves (links from this index)
  3. The Operator I reviewer scorecard (one per reviewer, filled independently)
  4. The Bootstrap Reviewer onboarding kit — only on first cycle they review

No other documents are required for the reviewer to do their job. If a reviewer asks for "background context," they have not read the onboarding kit yet.
