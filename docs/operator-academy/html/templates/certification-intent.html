Self-Assessment Against the Target Tier's Eight Requirements

Per the framework's Sections 2–5. The candidate's honest self-rating, 0–4 scale, with one-line defense per item.

Requirement Self-rating One-line defense
1. Required competencies (34-competency model) 0–4

5. Required business outcomes 0–4
6. Required role-play assessments 0–4

If the candidate rates themselves below 2 on any requirement, the Academy lead may decline to schedule certification until that gap is closed. That is the correct outcome — schedule wisely.
