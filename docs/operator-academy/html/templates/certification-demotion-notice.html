Demotion Notice

Used at Step 9 of the framework (Demotion Process). The Academy lead's formal written notice to an Operator that their certification is being demoted or revoked.

Demotion is rare. Demotion is also non-negotiable when warranted. This form's purpose is to ensure the decision is documented, defensible, and proceeds with dignity for both the institution and the Operator.

Operator's Right to Respond

Per Framework Section 9:

"The Operator has 30 days to respond and present counter-evidence."

The Operator's response should be in writing, attached to this notice. The Operator may:

  • Acknowledge and accept the proposed decision
  • Present counter-evidence (with specifics)
  • Request reconciliation conversation with the Academy lead before final decision
  • Request a second senior reviewer (per Framework Section 9)

A note on this template's use

Demotion is the framework's hardest moment. The template should be filled out completely. Shortcuts here erode the credential's defensibility — both for the Operator being demoted and for every other Operator whose certification depends on the framework's integrity.

If an Academy lead finds themselves filling this out frequently, that is a signal that something earlier in the process — selection, training, mentorship, or initial certification bar — has slipped. The forensic question is the same one Operators ask of customer issues: what system is producing these results?
