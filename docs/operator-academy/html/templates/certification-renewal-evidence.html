Renewal Evidence

Used at the certification renewal cycle (annual for Operator I + II; biennial for Operator III + Executive Advisor).

Renewal is evidence-based, like initial certification. Showing up does not renew the credential. Recent work does.

The renewal pack is smaller than the initial pack. It's a focused proof that the Operator has maintained the bar over the renewal period.
