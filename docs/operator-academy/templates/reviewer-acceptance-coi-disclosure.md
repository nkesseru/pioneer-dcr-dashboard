# Reviewer Acceptance + Conflict-of-Interest Disclosure

Filled by **each reviewer** at the start of each certification cycle they participate in. One form per reviewer per cycle.

**Why this form exists:** the [Bootstrap Reviewer onboarding kit](./bootstrap-reviewer-onboarding-kit.md) and [framework §7 Step 3](../operator-certification-framework.md) require independence. Independence is only meaningful if relationships are disclosed in writing before reviewing begins.

**When it's filled:** within 72 hours of receiving the reviewer ask. Before the candidate submits any evidence.

**Where it's filed:** appended to the cycle record file (`certification-cycles/cycle-{N}-{candidate}.md`) at decision time. Reviewers keep their own copy.

## What this form is NOT

- **Not new doctrine.** Operationalizes the existing independence requirement in framework §7 Step 3 and the Bootstrap clause §7.5 (Triple-Reviewer Safeguard).
- **Not a disqualification gate.** Most disclosures result in "proceed" or "proceed with memo." Only material, undisclosable conflicts (active financial entanglement, romantic relationship) disqualify.
- **Not negotiable.** A reviewer who refuses to disclose cannot review. No exceptions.
