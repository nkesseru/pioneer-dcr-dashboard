# Certification Self-Assessment

The candidate rates themselves on every competency in the [34-competency model](../00-academy-overview/02-competency-model.md), using the 0–3 scale from the model:

- **0** — Unaware
- **1** — Aware (can describe; cannot do)
- **2** — Competent (can do solo on typical customer with supervision available)
- **3** — Expert (can do solo on strategic customer AND can teach it to someone at level 1)

The candidate's self-rating is one of three inputs the reviewers use. Reviewers may rate higher or lower based on evidence. The candidate's honesty here — including rating themselves DOWN where appropriate — is itself an evidence signal.

This form is required for every tier certification AND every renewal.
