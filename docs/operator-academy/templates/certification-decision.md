# Certification Decision

Used at **Step 4** of the certification process. The Academy lead's formal decision after reviewing the candidate's evidence pack + both reviewers' independent evaluations.

This decision is the record. It is delivered to the candidate, archived in the registry, and referenced at every renewal.

## Academy Lead's Independent Assessment

The Academy lead is NOT a third reviewer. The Academy lead's job is to:

1. Verify the reviewers were calibrated
2. Verify the candidate's evidence pack was Pack-Acceptable (per evidence-pack template Step 4)
3. Apply qualitative judgment to the cumulative picture
4. Make the final decision

| Question | Academy lead's answer |
|---|---|
| Were both reviewers fully independent (no consultation before submission)? | Yes / No |
| Was the candidate's pack Pack-Acceptable before grading? | Yes / No |
| Do the reviewer ratings + Pioneer Test outcomes + Must Not check align? | Yes / No |
| Does the Academy lead's qualitative read align with the reviewers'? | Yes / No |

## The Decision

The Academy lead selects exactly one of the four outcomes:

| Decision | When applies |
|---|---|
| **Pass** | All sections ≥ 2 from both reviewers + Pioneer Test pass + no Must Not violations + qualitative judgment aligns |
| **Conditional Pass** | 6 of 8 sections ≥ 2 from both reviewers + Pioneer Test pass + one specific gap to close in 30 days |
| **No Pass — Gap-Driven** | 2+ sections below 2 from either reviewer, OR Pioneer Test fail. 30-day remediation plan with explicit bars. |
| **No Pass — Disqualifying** | Any Must Not violation, OR only-wins evidence record (curation flag). 60–90-day remediation OR role-fit conversation. |

## Re-Review Eligibility

Per framework Section 7.5: the candidate may request a single re-review by an additional senior reviewer within 14 days of decision delivery, if they believe the decision was incorrect.

## Appended — Decision Letter Template (Candidate-Facing)

The Academy lead sends a separate, candidate-friendly letter summarizing the decision. Suggested structure:

```
[Date]

Dear [Candidate],

Thank you for submitting your certification intent on [intent date]. I've now
reviewed your evidence pack, both reviewer evaluations, and applied the
Pioneer Test myself.

My decision is [outcome].

[For pass: one specific thing you did exceptionally well, named.]
[For conditional pass: the specific gap, the specific bar to close it,
 the date.]
[For no pass: specific bars not met, the path back, the timeline.]

Per framework Section 6 [Cross-Tier Requirements], one thing I want
you to carry into your next [quarter / certification window]:

[One specific piece of coaching.]

[Right to re-review reminder if applicable.]

[Renewal schedule if pass.]

In Pioneer's service,
[Academy lead name]
```

The candidate-facing letter is **not** a template to be filled out mechanically. It is a writing exercise the Academy lead performs once per decision. Generic letters undermine the framework's integrity.
