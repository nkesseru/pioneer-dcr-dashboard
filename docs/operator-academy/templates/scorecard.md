# Weekly Scorecard

Filled in every Friday by the operator. Reviewed in the Friday debrief.

The scorecard is the operator's running record of what they delivered + how they delivered it. Not a performance management tool. A coaching tool.

| Metric | Op I | Op II | Senior |
|---|---|---|---|
| Health avg | 65 | 75 | 80 |
| TTV days | ≤ 30 | ≤ 21 | ≤ 14 |
| QBRs / quarter | 3 | 8 | 12 |

If you're consistently above your tier's bar, talk about promotion. If you're consistently below, talk about coaching.
