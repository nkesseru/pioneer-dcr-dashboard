# Evidence Pack Cover Sheet

Used at **Step 2** of the certification process. The candidate compiles their evidence pack as a single shared folder; this cover sheet is the top-level index that two reviewers + the Academy lead use to navigate it.

Per Section 0, Principle 2 of the framework: *Evidence is the instrument of certification.* This pack IS the certification. Its quality determines the credential's defensibility.

## Honest Failures (Required)

Per the framework, an evidence record containing only successes is **disqualifying at every tier**.

The candidate names at least three honest failures from their certification period: prediction misses, actions that didn't verify, customers who churned despite the candidate's work, Loops that went stale, role-play attempts that didn't pass.

## For Reviewer Use — Pack Acceptability

A pack is **acceptable for grading** when all of the following are true:

- [ ] All universal items present
- [ ] All target-tier-specific items present
- [ ] At least three honest failures documented
- [ ] Pioneer Test self-application completed
- [ ] Candidate's closing statement is the candidate's voice (not AI-generated boilerplate)
- [ ] Business outcomes summary fully filled in with actual numbers

A pack failing any of these checks is returned for completion, not graded. The candidate may resubmit; the certification window may shift.
