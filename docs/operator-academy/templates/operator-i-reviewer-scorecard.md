# Operator I Reviewer Scorecard

One reviewer fills one scorecard. **No coordination with other reviewers before submitting.** Calibration happens after all three scorecards are submitted, not during.

## Scoring scale (per category)

- **Pass** — meets the bar, evidence is reviewer-defensible
- **Conditional** — meets most of the bar, ≤ 2 specific gaps that could close in 14 days
- **Not Yet** — does not meet the bar at this time

Reviewer must cite specific evidence for any Pass. "Looked good" is not a citation.
