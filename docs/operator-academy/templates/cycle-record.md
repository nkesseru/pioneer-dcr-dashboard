# Cycle Record — Template

**Copy this template to `certification-cycles/cycle-{N}-{candidate-slug}-{tier}.md` at the start of every cycle.** Fill incrementally; finalize within 48 hours of the calibration meeting.

This is the **permanent institutional record** of the cycle. Used by:
- Future Operators researching how cycles ran
- Future reviewers calibrating their own scoring
- Renewal reviewers checking the original decision basis
- Auditors and researchers studying the Academy's evolution

Every cycle produces one. No cycle exists without a record.

## What this record is NOT

- **Not the decision letter to the candidate.** That's [`certification-decision.md`](./certification-decision.md), separate, delivered within 24 hours of the calibration meeting.
- **Not the calibration record.** That's [`calibration-meeting-record.md`](./calibration-meeting-record.md), attached here.
- **Not editable after archive date.** Errors are corrected by appending a v1.1 addendum, not editing the original.
- **Not a marketing artifact.** This is operational truth. Wins and losses both go in, with names and numbers.
