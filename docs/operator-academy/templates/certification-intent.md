Used at **Step 1** of the certification process per the [Operator Certification Framework](../operator-certification-framework.md). Submitted by the candidate, co-signed by their direct manager, sent to the Academy lead.

If the Academy lead reviewing this declines to schedule certification, that is NOT a no-pass. It is a "not ready to evaluate yet" signal. Address the gaps and resubmit.
