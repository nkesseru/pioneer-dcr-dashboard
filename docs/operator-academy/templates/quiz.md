# Quiz Template

Short assessment for a lesson or pillar. 10 questions max. Should take an operator 15–20 minutes.

Used for:
- End-of-lesson spot check
- End-of-pillar check
- Pre-certification self-test
- Trainer calibration ("am I teaching this right?")

Quizzes are not pass-or-fail in isolation. They're a signal for coaching.

---

## Quiz title

> Example: *01.2 Activation & TTV — End of Lesson*

## Pillar / Lesson covered

> Example: Pillar 01 — Lesson 01.2

## Target operator tier

> Apprentice / Operator I / Operator II / Senior

## Time

> 20 minutes

## Passing bar

> 7 of 10 = pass
> 5–6 of 10 = repeat the lesson, then retake
> 4 or fewer = trainer review; significant gap

---

## Format mix (recommended)

Use a mix. Pure multiple-choice quizzes test recall, not judgment. Operators need both.

| Type | Count | What it tests |
|---|---|---|
| Multiple choice | 3–4 | Concept recall |
| Short answer (1–2 sentences) | 2–3 | Understanding |
| Scenario | 2–3 | Application |
| Edge case | 1 | Judgment |

---

## Scoring

| Score | What to do |
|---|---|
| 9–10 | Pass. Operator is at level 2+ for the competency. |
| 7–8 | Pass with notes. Review the missed questions. |
| 5–6 | No pass. Repeat the lesson. Retake within 1 week. |
| 0–4 | No pass. Trainer review. Likely a curriculum gap or coaching gap, not just an operator gap. |
