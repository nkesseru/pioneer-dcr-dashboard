# Operator I Launch Plan — Generic Path

**The objective** (now generic).  An Operator I candidate enters the Academy as an Apprentice. Six weeks later, they are either certified as a real Operator I or have a documented decision for why not.

**Cycle 1 (Nick Kesseru, Founder Pilot):** target start 2026-06-15, target decision 2026-07-24. See [`founder-pilot-plan.md`](./founder-pilot-plan.md).

## Why Operator I, not Operator II

The original Mock Cycle 1 design targeted April at Operator II directly. That was the wrong bar for a first cycle.

- **Operator II requires 8 delivered QBRs, NRR ≥ 105%, 12 Loop entries, recorded executive briefings, implementation case studies.** Most of that doesn't exist in archival form today.
- **Operator I requires 8 Vital Reads, 3 Loop entries (1 closed), 1 Customer Economics analysis, 1 verified business outcome.** Most of that can be produced *prospectively* in 6 weeks.

Operator I is the foundational credential. Producing one of these first proves the system works. Then Operator II is the next cycle, not the first one.

## 8. What would fail certification?

Per Framework v1.1, automatic fail conditions:

| Trigger | Source |
|---|---|
| Any of the 4 quizzes < 7/10 *and* candidate refuses re-take | Framework §2 |
| Zero closed Loops (only open entries, no verifications honored) | Framework §2 "doctrine pivot" — the 1-closed-Loop bar |
| Customer Economics analysis arithmetic fails Pillar 7 inspection | Constitution Pillar 7 |
| No verified business outcome on an owned account | Framework §2 "Required business outcomes" |
| Pioneer Test reflection fails the Pioneer Test (vague, not measurable, no honest failure) | Constitution §"Pioneer Test" |
| Fails 2 of 2 chosen roleplays | Framework §2 "Required role-play assessments" |
| Self-rating shows any of the 34 competencies at level 0 | Framework §2 "Crystal-clear bar" |
| Less than 15 of 34 competencies at level 2 or higher | Framework §2 |

**Soft fail signals** (calibration discussion, may convert to Conditional Pass):
- Vital Read recordings exist but candidate dominated the conversation rather than letting CEO read.
- Loop closure was technically logged but the "verification" was loose.
- 1 quiz was retaken to 7/10 after first attempt at 5/10.

## 9. What would pass certification?

**Hard pass:** every item in §4 (evidence pack) present, every item in §6 (practical exercises) at bar, all reviewers score the candidate at "ready" or above. Lineage ID `POPS-OP-I-2026-001` issued.

**Conditional pass:** evidence pack present but ≤ 2 specific gaps named (e.g., business outcome metric still soft, or 1 Vital Read recording lost). Candidate given 14 days to remediate. If remediated, full pass; if not, Not Yet.

**Not Yet:** more than 2 gaps OR any hard-fail condition triggered. Candidate stays Apprentice. Cycle 2 begins after 4 weeks of additional work. Not a personal verdict — a signal that the evidence isn't yet there.

## 10. What is the fastest realistic path to Operator I?

**6 weeks.** Locked by two physical constraints:

1. **The 4-consecutive-week Vital Read requirement.** Cannot be compressed without violating framework §2. That's 4 weeks floor.
2. **Loop verification dates must elapse.** A Loop with a 7-day verification date created Week 2 can be closed Week 3. The single closed Loop is the binding requirement; cannot fake-close before verification window elapses.

The remaining 2 weeks cover (a) onboarding/reading/quizzes and (b) reviewer reads + decision. The reviewer phase could theoretically compress to 1 week if all three reviewers move fast.

**Could it be 5 weeks?** Maybe. Requires:
- April reads all 4 lessons + Constitution in Week 0 (before Monday).
- Customer Economics analysis drafted on Day 1 of Week 1.
- Loop verification windows set to 5 days instead of 7.
- Reviewer reads compressed to 4 days, calibration on Friday of Week 5.

**Could it be 4 weeks?** Not without violating the framework. The 4-week consecutive Vital Read floor is not negotiable. The doctrine bar exists to prove sustained delivery, not one good week.

**Recommendation: target 6 weeks (2026-07-27 decision day). Optimize for honesty, not speed.** A Cycle 1 that takes 7 weeks but produces a real Operator I beats a 5-week cycle that requires Framework v1.2 amendments to justify what was skipped.
