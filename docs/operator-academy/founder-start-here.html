Founder feedback checklist

You're not just running the cycle — you're auditing the Academy as you go. Capture these as you hit them. They feed Laura's cycle, not yours.

Friction surfaced

What made you think "ugh, this is awkward"? Was it a missing template, a confusing workflow, a step that took 3× longer than it should have?

Gaps found

What did you need that didn't exist? What did you have to improvise? Capture even small ones — Laura won't have your context to improvise from.

Surprises (positive)

What landed better than expected? Which artifact actually helped? Which workflow was clearer than you remembered writing it?

Surprises (negative)

What felt fine on paper but didn't survive contact with real work? Where did the doctrine sound right but the operation broke?

Time estimates

Where did you over- or under-estimate? The fast-track says ~18 hours for the founder. If reality is 24 hours, Laura's projected 30 is probably 40+. Update assumptions.

Things to tell Laura specifically

What advice would you give Laura before her Day 0 that you wish you'd had on yours? Those become the next version of her launch page.

Framework v1.2 candidates

What doctrine, framework, or template would you actually change? Defer the change itself until Cycle 1 closes — but capture the candidate now while it's fresh.

Recommend a single markdown file: founder-pilot-feedback-log.md at the repo root, captured daily in 2–3 sentences per category.
