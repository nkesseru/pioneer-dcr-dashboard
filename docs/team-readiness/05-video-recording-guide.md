# Deliverable 5 — Video Recording Guide

A recommended sequence for Nick (or whoever's recording) to capture one continuous screen recording that walks a new cleaning tech through their entire daily workflow.

**Goal:** A 25–35 minute video that a brand-new tech can watch ONCE and then start working independently the same day.

**Recording setup:**
- Resolution: 1920 × 1080 (full HD). Browser zoom at 100%.
- Tool: QuickTime / Loom / OBS — whatever you're fastest in. Loom is easiest to share.
- Audio: external mic if possible. Built-in is fine if you can do a quiet take.
- Mouse cursor: visible, default size. Click animations on if your tool supports it.
- Screen: SOLO browser window. Close Slack, email, notifications. Use a fresh Chrome profile if possible to avoid showing real customer data.

**Identity setup (do this BEFORE recording):**
- Sign in as a designated "training" tech account if you have one. If not, blur or skip sections that show identifiable customer data.
- Have a real test customer assigned for today.
- Pre-create one pending time adjustment so the My Hours walkthrough shows the badge.
- Pre-stage a leadership message so /team-hub has something to demo.

---

## Recording Sequence

The script blocks below are speaking notes. Read them at normal pace; don't read every word — paraphrase. The intent is a confident, friendly walkthrough that doesn't feel scripted.

### Block 0 — Cold open (30 sec)

**On screen:** Sign-in page at `https://pioneer-dcr-hub.web.app/team-hub`.

**Say:** "Hey team, this is Nick. This video is your start-to-finish walkthrough of PioneerOps — what you'll use every day. By the end of this video, you'll be able to clock in, do your work, submit a DCR, and verify you got paid right. Total time: about 30 minutes. Let's go."

---

### Block 1 — Signing in (2 min)

**On screen:** Sign-in page.

**Demonstrate:** Click **Continue with Google**. Authenticate. Land on `/team-hub`.

**Say:**
- "Use this email and ONLY this email. The whole system identifies you by this address — switching emails is how things break."
- "If you get a 'Restricted' page, it means we haven't enabled your account yet. Call Kirby."
- "Bookmark this URL: team-hub. This is your home page."

---

### Block 2 — Team Hub tour (3 min)

**On screen:** `/team-hub` fully loaded.

**Demonstrate:** Scroll top to bottom, pausing on each section.

**Say:**
- "When you sign in, this is the order to read each section: Leadership first, Messages next, Announcements third, then your hours."
- "From Leadership — short notes from April or Nick. Click Acknowledge to dismiss, Reply if it's a real question."
- "Messages from Pioneer — these are conversations with Kirby. You can reply."
- "Announcements — anything mandatory pops up as a modal until you acknowledge it. Don't ignore them."
- "Skip ahead to My Hours — we'll come back to it after we've actually clocked in."

---

### Block 3 — Today's work (3 min)

**On screen:** Open new tab → `/work`.

**Demonstrate:** Show today's assigned customer cards.

**Say:**
- "This is /work — your daily job board. You'll see one card per customer assigned to you today."
- "If you don't see anything, that's a problem — message Kirby on the Need Help Now card."
- "Tap a card to see the job details. Pay attention to start time, end time, customer access codes, special instructions."

**Demonstrate:** Tap a customer card. Show the SOP / access info.

---

### Block 4 — Clock In + cleaning (2 min)

**On screen:** Customer card.

**Demonstrate:** Tap **Clock In**. Show the green active state. Show the timer.

**Say:**
- "When you arrive at the customer site, you clock in. Just tap that button."
- "Notice the page turns green and the timer starts. That's how you know you're on the clock."
- "Now you do your work. We'll fast-forward."

**Cut/jump:** Show ~3 seconds of black overlay or fast-forward effect. Resume on the same screen with the timer showing more elapsed time.

---

### Block 5 — Clock Out + DCR (8 min)

**On screen:** Customer card with active timer.

**Demonstrate:** Tap **Clock Out**. DCR form opens.

**Say:**
- "When you finish, tap Clock Out. The DCR form opens automatically — you have to fill this out before you leave."
- "DCR stands for Daily Cleaning Report. It tells Pioneer + the customer what you cleaned."

**Demonstrate each section slowly:**
- Bathrooms — mark each item Yes / No / N/A. Demonstrate with realistic clicks.
- General Areas — same pattern.
- Kitchens / Break Rooms — same.
- Offices — same.
- Entryways — same.

**Say:**
- "Be honest. If something wasn't applicable today, mark N/A. If you didn't get to it, mark No and add a note about why. The customer would rather know."
- "Photos — at least one per section is the Pioneer expectation. Doesn't have to be art. Quality > quantity."

**Demonstrate:** Add a sample photo (or simulate).

- "Issues at the end — this is where you tell Kirby about anything broken or running low. It creates a follow-up task for her."

**Demonstrate:** Type your name in signature. Click Submit DCR. Show the success screen.

**Say:**
- "Done. Your shift is now complete and the customer gets an email summarizing what you did."

---

### Block 6 — My Hours self-check (5 min)

**On screen:** Back to `/team-hub`. Scroll to My Hours.

**Demonstrate:** The pre-staged pending adjustment makes the chip yellow. Walk through:
- Confidence chip (state)
- Period dates + "Payroll closes:" date
- Total hours tile
- Pending corrections tile (your pre-staged one)
- Recent 5 shifts list
- View All Shifts toggle

**Say:**
- "Every shift you've worked this pay period shows up here. Your total is what you'll get paid for."
- "The chip at the top — green means hours look good; yellow means you have a correction pending."
- "If a shift looks wrong — wrong time, wrong customer, anything — click Adjust on that row."

**Demonstrate Time Adjustment flow:**
- Click Adjust on a shift
- Modal opens, show the pre-filled times
- Change one time slightly
- Pick a reason (e.g., "Forgot to clock out")
- Type a note: "Left at 9:15 PM but didn't clock out until I got home."
- Submit Request
- Show the success message + the "Pending correction" badge appearing

**Say:**
- "Kirby will review and either approve it or follow up with you. Don't worry about the correction tile turning yellow — that's how you KNOW it's in the system."
- "If you ever look at your hours and total seems low, it's usually a shift missing a clock-out. Submit the correction. Done."

---

### Block 7 — Requests & Support (4 min)

**On screen:** Scroll to Requests & Support grid.

**Demonstrate each tile (briefly):**

**Say:**
- "Need Help Now — for the 'I'm stuck right now' moments. Goes straight to Kirby."
- "Time Off — for planned days off, schedule in advance. Kirby approves."
- "Call Out — for 'I can't make my shift tonight.' Same-day. Don't text Kirby, use this."
- "Open Shifts — if you want extra hours, claim a shift someone else couldn't cover. There's a Rockstar bonus if you successfully cover."
- "Improve — got an idea for how Pioneer could be better? Drop it here."
- "Safety Incident — anything dangerous, hurt yourself, customer property damage. Document it immediately."

**Demonstrate:** Click into Call Out as an example. Show the form. Don't actually submit.

---

### Block 8 — Supply Pickup (3 min)

**On screen:** Open `/supply-station`.

**Demonstrate:**
- Show the Supply Pickup hero card at the top
- Show the access codes (blur them if your test profile has real ones)
- Click **Start Supply Pickup** to demonstrate the clock
- Scroll to the supply request form
- Show how to pick customer + add items
- Click **Complete Supply Pickup** to demonstrate end

**Say:**
- "When you go to the storage unit, start the Pickup clock. You're getting paid for that time."
- "Use the supply request form to tell Kirby what to order. Be specific — brand, size."
- "When you leave the unit, complete the Pickup. Done."

---

### Block 9 — Off-limits surfaces (1 min)

**On screen:** Try to open `/admin` or `/manager`.

**Demonstrate:** Show the "Restricted" or "Inspections are admin-only" page.

**Say:**
- "You might see links to /admin, /manager, /inspections, /ceo somewhere. If you click them and see 'Restricted,' that's working as intended. Those are admin pages."
- "Don't try to work around it. If you think you need access, talk to Kirby."

---

### Block 10 — Wrap-up (2 min)

**On screen:** Back to `/team-hub`.

**Say:**
- "That's it. Your daily flow: sign in, read leadership + announcements, go to /work, clock in, do the job, clock out + DCR, check My Hours later."
- "If something breaks, use Need Help Now. If you forget anything from this video, message Kirby — she'd rather answer twice than have you guess."
- "Welcome to Pioneer."

---

## Editing notes

- **Cuts to keep:** Block 0 cold open, every demo click, the success screens.
- **Cuts to trim:** Loading spinners > 2 seconds, any moment you mouse around without explaining.
- **Cuts to redo:** Any moment you say "uh, hold on" or click the wrong thing twice. One re-take section beats a confusing original.
- **End-card:** Show your name + "Questions? text Kirby at [number]" + "Bookmark: pioneer-dcr-hub.web.app/team-hub"

## Recommended output

- 1080p MP4
- ~30 min length (trim heavily if longer; tech attention drops past 30)
- Stored in a shared Drive folder labeled "PioneerOps Walkthrough — v1 [date]"
- Linked from the Onboarding section of `/admin → Training` (when training surfaces are updated to reference it)
