B. Deputy Companies → Pioneer customers

—

Every Deputy shift carries a Company.Id in _DPMetaData.OperationalUnitInfo. Map each Deputy company to a Pioneer customer once and every future shift auto-resolves with matchSource: "deputy_company_id".
