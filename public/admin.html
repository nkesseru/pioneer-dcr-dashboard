A. Connection Health

—

Unmapped Deputy people

—

One row per Deputy person we've seen who isn't yet linked to a Pioneer cleaning tech. Map once; the link persists for every future shift.

B. Deputy Companies → Pioneer customers

—

Every Deputy shift carries a Company.Id in _DPMetaData.OperationalUnitInfo. Map each Deputy company to a Pioneer customer once and every future shift auto-resolves with matchSource: "deputy_company_id".

C. Advanced / Debug (collapsed)

Aliases are now supplemental — they only fire when no deputy_company_id is set on the Pioneer customer doc for the shift's Deputy company. Prefer mapping Deputy Companies above; use this panel only for edge cases (one-off shorthand codes, testing, or migration from older alias data).
